About $MYNK

Many may ask, what is Myro? Myro is the name of the dog
owned by Raj Gokal, one of the co-founders of Solana. This
project pays homage to him, and his dog, and responds to the
popular demand for dog-based narratives in the crypto
space. Myro was created in response to this demand, and to
provide value to the ecosystem created by Raj and his partner
— the Solana blockchain.

Myro is more than just a cryptocurrency, it is also a
movement. We believe that Solana has the potential to
change the world for the better, and we are committed to
making it more inclusive and welcoming for everyone.
